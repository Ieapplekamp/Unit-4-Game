Power Stones!

There will be no cheating, with that being said, no calculators!

To win the game is fairly straight forward, your objective is to match the target number. Failure to the reach the target number will results in a loss. Each stone will hold a random value, and only 4 of the 6 stones will positively impact your goal.

Note: There are two special stones!
  • One to correct mistakes
  • One to test your odds if all else fails
